1/**
2 * @file ISensor.h
3 * @brief Common interface implemented by every sensor of the station.
4 *
5 * This is the extension point of the firmware: adding a new sensor means
6 * writing one class that implements ISensor and registering it in the
7 * SensorManager — main.cpp, the JSON payload assembly and the LoRa link
8 * are not touched.
9 *
10 * Contract:
11 * - begin() is called once per wake-up cycle, before any read();
12 * - read() writes the sensor's own fields into the telemetry JSON object
13 * (see docs/lora-protocol.md for field names and units);
14 * - on failure the sensor simply does not add its fields: per protocol,
15 * absent fields mean "sensor unavailable" and the receiver must cope.
16 */
17

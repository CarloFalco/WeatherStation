1/**
2 * @file RainGauge.h
3 * @brief Tipping-bucket rain gauge (reed switch, pulse counting).
4 *
5 * Contributes the "rain" [mm] field: rainfall accumulated since the last
6 * transmission. Pulses are counted in two ways:
7 * - while awake: GPIO interrupt with software debounce;
8 * - while in deep sleep: the pulse itself wakes the MCU (EXT0), main.cpp
9 * increments the counter through countSleepPulse() and goes straight
10 * back to sleep (quick path, no radio, no sensors).
11 *
12 * The counter lives in RTC RAM (RtcState::rainPulses) so no rain is lost
13 * across deep-sleep cycles. Calibration (mm per bucket tip) comes from
14 * config.ini: [rain] mm_per_pulse.
15 */
16

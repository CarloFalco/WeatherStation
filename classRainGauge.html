◆ resetAccumulator()

void RainGauge::resetAccumulator ( )

Consume the pulses that were reported by the last read().

Call after a confirmed delivery (base ACK received). Subtracts the snapshot taken by read() instead of zeroing the counter, so bucket tips that occurred during the transmission/ACK window are carried over to the next cycle instead of being lost.

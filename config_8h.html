Detailed Description

Hardware configuration: GPIO pinout, I2C addresses, board constants.

Single source of truth for the wiring of the station. Runtime-tunable parameters (wake interval, station ID, LoRa settings, ...) do NOT belong here: they live in /config.ini on LittleFS (see AppConfig, Increment 1).
